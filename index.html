ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Dariusz Markowicz and I am 25 years old. I am a university graduate, and I studied economics and computer science. I am a self-motivated, responsible and hard working person. I am a mature worker and adaptable to challenging situations, both in a team environment as well as using my own initiative.

The reason why I started studying economics was that I wanted to start my own company in the design/construction industry. However, it changed at the beginning of 2014. When I started to be more interested in IT. A circumstance that contributed to my interest in this industry was reading about the shortage of specialists in this field in the coming years (I asked myself - maybe this is something for me?). In those days, I knew almost nothing about programming languages. Earlier, it happened that I was writing a page on Joomla or an HTML page during classes in Computer Science in high school. As I mentioned, my curiosity and satisfaction caused me to start writing programs. The first ones were in C. It was fascinating - to execute instructions on the console. I decided to change my field of study - with a bit of luck I passed the entrance examination, and at the same time I started a post-secondary vocational school with the profile of IT technician. To bring the computer world closer to me.Later, I learned C ++ as well as web technologies. Currently, I focus on the second direction because it gives me the most satisfaction. I took seriously to learn and did not touch frameworks and libraries or templates until I learned the basics well. I gave myself time to learn how to write code. So, I take two courses in programming: one direction allows me to understand what programming is, while front-end is for me what I want to do professionally. Here are the projects that I have realized, to develop myself in this field: obscurab.us , and the chess game .

I am building below website based on current trends in web design, but I am trying to apply my own interaction solution with the user, inspired by other websites - it allows me to maintain my enthusiasm and branden my expertise in the field.

Currently, my training focuses on React. Systematically, I gain knowledge and skills on technologies: AWS Cloud9, Mongo, Express by writing the TO-DO LIST application with its own API. Other issues are: ECMAScript2015-2017, SPA, D3, JSX, Virtual DOM, Authentication, React Router, Redux.
